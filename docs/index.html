Cosmology

There are two forces - Torment (antipode) and Light (deity). They are equal in their strength, eternity and aspirations. Deity and Torment act together and cannot live without each other.

The main world was formed as a result of the contact of these two forces: the Torment literally tore off pieces of the Divine, and their fusion gave something in between - which became the world in which everything happens. And since it was the Torment that tore it off, it reigns in the world. It's under Torment's control, BUT cannot completely merge with it, because the divine principle is still present.

The Divine wants its piece back, but cannot accept the generation of Torment. So every being (and the world as a whole) must be cleansed through its suffering.

Purification can drag on for several rebirths (and therefore a LOT of people are unhappy, because they haven't done anything wrong, but are doomed, possibly to eternal rebirth without the possibility of salvation). Rebirths work like in Buddhism: If you behaved well, you go in a better direction, if not — in the opposite.

Creature can be reborn as everything: from plants to the undead. The only thing you cannot become is a divine creature, because they are determined.

The Deity is not depicted in any way other than as a coming light. Among all peoples there is no culture of depicting a deity in any form other than as a scattered ray. This is not so much a prohibition (although it exists), but rather a general conviction that something so high and distant from their sinful world cannot have the usual forms, much less take the form of low things.

History

In the silence of torment, the echoes of our inner battles resound louder than any thunder...

The world has existed for a long time, but endless suffering absorbs everything rational. Periodically, the countdown of time is lost, as is the creature's faith in its salvation.

All time is counted from the “First Torment” - what the Divine had to endure in order for the world to appear. And even though history has been rewritten and changed many times, this event is determined in every mind, because it is their progenitor. Every creature that has even a grain of light in itself remembers the “First Torment” as the moment of its own fall.

Everyone living in the world recognizes their weakness before the Divine. And they know that even if they change history, then the Hegemon - the Divine messenger - has everything written out and determined as needed.

“The Chronicle of the Hegemon” — an idea of history. The belief that all fate is recorded on the scrolls of the Hegemon (the consubstantial guardian of time and the course of events) The myth is depicted differently in different places. There is no single canon of her image, but as a rule it is a young girl with an endless scroll and a feather. Only an apostate does not recognize the Hegemon's power over time and fate, and every believer longs for a meeting and the opportunity to find his own verse in the chronicle.

The main plot that Emrys will carry on his shoulders takes place in verse 944 from “The First Torment,” and his difficult fate has already been written by the Hegemon's pen…

Genealogy

Main Characters:

Emrys

In the shadows and oppression, in the land of Torment, the hero of our story was born. He was part of a humble human family. His parents were ardent followers of the Deity, their faith was their pillar and the reason for their resilience in the face of adversity.Surrounded by the gloomy expanse and the heavy burden of suffering, his parents endeavoured to find light in the darkness that permeated Torment.They died because of their faith by the hands of the guards of Torment, and never even waited for the coming of Diety.

The hero was left alone, deprived of support and defence, in a world consumed by agony. By the standards of the time of the world of Torment for our years, he was about 17 years old. With no hope of salvation and no one to help him, he faced a choice: stay in a world where suffering was the only reality, or try to find his way to the light his parents believed in and set him free.

Obsessed with the idea of liberation and the search for salvation, the hero sets out in search of the so-called "saviours" he has heard so much about from his parents. Driven by the hope that the light is still out there, he embarks on a journey, searching for his way to the light in the depths of a world shrouded in Misery.

Ermys' search for a savior led to an encounter with Girsus the Blatant, who was said to know the way to the deity, but this savior was a loyal servant of Torment and had the least interest in anyone finding true salvation from torment. Instead, he decided to use the hero as another example of a "fool" for the salvation-believing public.

He gave him a path that supposedly should lead him to the Deity, full of trials and sufferings beyond the power of any living creature. And it was beyond him. He encountered probably powerful dark forces and was swallowed by them. But for his suffering and faith according to the system of rebirth the guy was reborn into a demi-deity.

False Saviours:

Girsus the Blatant

It is one of the first and still living false saviors.

Once he felt the taste of the filth that obscures the mind, he forever became its helper. His longevity is justified by the fact that his service is profitable and useful to the Torment, since the networks organized by his apostate cause lead more people into oblivion. His appearance is not attractive, but this was not always the case.

Long years of service to the Torment had drained all humanity from his face. Its good that messengers are supposed to hide it...

Vistula the Lack-willed

The false savior's assistants change from year to year, but Emrys gets to meet some of them:

- Bezees the Hiding - is the right hand, the main assistant and the first of the crowd of those who hope for salvation through false salvation. He will reveal their ideology to Emrys and teach them how to serve. Alas, he himself will become the first victim to save Emrys and will fall at his hands.

- Vistula the Lack-willed - is a coercive apostate, who in fact has the goal of serving the light, but does it from behind enemy lines. By the will of the Deity, he will become Emris's spiritual mentor and guide, but not for long.. the Torment senses betrayal in his ranks...

Nature

Fauna

The fauna of Tormentum boasts a variety of creatures - the births of The Torment, those who were able to adapt and evolve. Species diversity begins with more or less familiar animals - artiodactyls, canines, birds, etc., but we must take into account that they were greatly influenced by the conditions in which they developed. Thus, the majority are distinguished by increased aggressiveness, a changed diet (some herbivores are able to taste flesh), and behavior in packs. And at the same time, The Torment absorbs sufferers into its darkness and gives birth to creatures similar to the undead, species acquire the most bizarre and unusual shapes, sizes and behavior: starting from something between a dead and a human-like and up to complete absorption and creatures of mysterious origin.

Dogs

There are also other animals in the world that are more familiar to us: wolves, birds, deer and so on. However, due to the nature of the world, as well as the influence of Torment.

Dust monsters

Dwell in any abandoned place: empty cities, scorched forests and drained water bodies. They absorb the energies of death and destruction, and favor lost humans and large animals as delicacies.

Wasteland & Desert worms

Dwell in dry regions, under sands and fossils. They hunt at night when small animals come to the cooled surface. The largest worms prey on wanderers.

Skull jellyfish

They live at the deepest depths and are hardly studied. Legend has it that deep-sea jellyfish absorb what remains of mermaid victims and create a horrible symbiosis

Mermaids

Inhabitants of water bodies. Drag to the bottom and kill travelers who have been wrecked. They smell blood from afar and swim to their victims. Their long tentacle tails allow them to move quickly and grab their victims.

Summoner or leshii

Half-dead creatures that were once endowed with reason, but have lost it over the centuries. Protect the forests and territories they consider their own from the prying eyes of travelers. If blood comes into contact with leshy moss, they will become infected. A person will gradually lose his mind, become covered with mold and moss, rot and eventually turn into the same half-dead creature.

Moths

Unpleasant creatures, though some find them enchanting. Not dangerous in themselves, but their parts (e.g. wings or sap) can be used to make quite dangerous poisons

Forest Fairies

They are slightly smaller than a human, have sharp teeth, sometimes horns or antennae-like outgrowths. They hunt small animals and birds, do not like travelers and can attack and seriously injure them.

Pixies and Doxy (small fairies)

Less intelligent fairies are small, like annoying pests. They can get attached to travelers, bite them, take away their belongings, and try to lead them into their lairs. A large group of fairies can seriously harm.

Flora

Some of the vegetation includes common forms such as trees, grass and shrubs. However, the conditions for their survival are very different: the lack of minerals and clean water in the soil makes it difficult for most plants to survive; trees rot before a leaf leaves, grass turns brown due to the blood soaked soil, and flowers cannot thrive in such a bleak environment. Yet, other, equally horrible plants thrive in these horrible conditions.
